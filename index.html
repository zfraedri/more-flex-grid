Welcome to Houseplant Hub!

Our plants are green and stuff. You know, like plants. It's pretty great. Do you like plants? They enjoy music and sunlight. Probably not long walks on the beach though. Don't let your pets eat houseplants, they tend to be poisonous. I mean that the houseplants are poisonous, not your pets. Yikes.

Our Plants

Below you can find a small selection of the plants we offer. Be sure to stop by to view our full selection at our brick and mortar location in Tempe, Arizona. We also offer classes, parties, and plant-sitting services.

Scary Boi

The monstera plant isn't really scary, unless your ceilings are low, I guess. They can grow to be 10 to 15 feet tall and 8 feet wide! It's like Little Shop of Horrors. Yikes again.

$20 - $100

Pokey Boi

There are many types of poke-y bois, especially here in the wilds of Arizona. I suggest avoiding the ones that jump at you. They're not the best.

$10 - $100

Sturdy Boi

The pothos plant will probably be around and thriving a million years from now. These things are like cockroaches. If you aren't the best with plants, this or a fake plant is best.
